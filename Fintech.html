Roll Rate and Loss Provisioning Report

Vintage Analysis and Forecasting

Summary

As a FinTech company it is imperative to closely monitor and manage the peformance of yout loan portfolio. To achieve this Generating a Roll Rate Report helps indentify trends and potential risks by analyzing the transition of loan between different payment statuses. The Loss Provisioning Report allows for the allocation of funds to cover potential losses enaching risk management and financial planning. Additonally conducting a Vintage Analysis Report aids in understanding the behavior of loans issued during specific periods enabling better informed decision making for future loan originations. These reports collectively provide a comprehensive view of laon performance ensuring proactive risj mitigation and support the overall health and sustainability of your lending activites.

Project Objectives

  • Create an automated sysytem for producing detailed reports on Roll Rates, Loss Provisioning and Vintage Analysis by leveraging loan data
  • Develop interactive dashboard and reports to visually represent the performance and credit risk of the loan portfolio.
  • Explore and understand the factors influencing loan default and evaluate credit risk across various segments and vintage cohort.

    • Approach

  • Data Scrutiny and Enhancement:Conducted a meticulous data cleaning and preprocessing phase to fortify the loan data's quality and uniformity. This involved purging duplicates, imputing missing data and standardizing formats to lay q robust foundation for subsequent analyses.
  • Roll Rate Exploration:Undertook a thorough roll rate analysis unveiling the percentage of borrowers defaulting during specific periods. The segmentation of the loan portfolio based on vintage loan type and pertinent criteria unravled nuanced pattrns and trends within the roll rates.
  • Loss Provisioning Estimation:Leverage insights from the roll rate report to project anticipated losses within the loan portfolio. Employed statistical models, segmened the portfolio by risk category and to derive precise estimates, contributing to a proactive and data driven loss provisioning strategy.
  • Vintage Performance Deconstruction:Engage in a granular examination of loan performance across different vintages, dissecting trends in credit quality.Through segmentation based on vintage and other pertinent factors deploy statistical models to discern the fundamental drivers behind loan defaults providing a holistic understanding of portfolio dynamics.

    • Conclusion

    Looking at the data from fintech loans using reports like roll rate, loss provisioning and vintage analysis has given a good understanding of the risks involved.The sysytem we created to generate these reports automatically has saved time and reduced mistakes compared to doing it manually.The way we present the loan data through interactive dashboard has made it eaiser for users to make decision about credit.
