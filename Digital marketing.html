Online Shopping Behaviour

Summary

The analysis of the google merchandise store focused on leveraging large datasets to understand customer online behaviour and their impact on profitability.By transforming data from session and hit level using Google Big Query into a relational table,a comprehensive overvirew of the online retail company's performance was achieved. The data,spanning one year and comprising over 1.4 billion records which provided valuable insights into key business dimensions.

Project Objectives

The primary objective of the project was to bridge gap between customer online behaviours and profitability for the Google Merchandise Store.This involved understanding and optimizing website traffic, analyzing traffic sources,assessing geographical variations,evaluating acquisition channels, and identifying the top performing pages.

Analysis Approach

Data Transformation:Large datasets from sessions and hit levels were transformed into a relational table using Google Big Query, enabling a more streamlined and accessible foramt for analysis.

Visualization:A Tableau dashboard ws designed to visualize key metrics across five business dimensions: website traffic,traffic source,traffic per location,acquisition per channel, and top-performing pages.

Key Metric Analysis:

Traffic Source:Identified that organic and YouTube were the top traffic sources, collectively contributing to over 50% of all website traffic.

Visitor Metrics:Noted that the website received 2 million unique visitor and 5 million pageviews during the evaluated period.

Conclusion

The analysis revealed crucial insights into the performance of the Google Merchandise Store. The dominance of the organic and YouTube as primary sources underscores the importance of these channels for the comapany.The Substantial number of unique visitors and pageview indicates a strong online presence.

Recommendations

1.Optimize Organic and Youtube Strategies:Given their significant contribution to website traffic, the company should further optimize its strategies for orgain and YouTube to maintain and enhance their effectiveness.

2.Enhance User Experience on Top Pages:Focus on improving the user experience on the top-performing pages to maximize engagement and potentially drive more conversions.

3.Geographical Targeting:Explore opportunities for targeted marketing and promotions based on geographical varations in traffic to enhance regional performance.

4.Diversity Acquisition Channels:While organic and YouTube are stong performers, consider diversifying acquistion channels to ensure a well-rounded and sustainable traffic strategy.

By implementing these recommendations the Google Merchandise Store can contribute to leverage its online presence,capitalize on successful channels and address areas for improvement ultimately driving increased profitability and customer satisfaction.
